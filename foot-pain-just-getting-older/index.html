When Foot Pain Becomes Limiting

Getting out of bed can be a grueling experience for many of us. The creaky joints, the stiff muscles, and of course, the tender feet. For Sarah, a 55-year-old grandmother, waking up to the sound of her alarm was just the beginning of a long day of dealing with chronic foot pain. She'd been feeling the ache in her heels and arches for months, but had resigned herself to the assumption that "everyone's feet hurt at this age, right?" It wasn't until she found herself struggling to keep up with her grandkids that she realized her foot pain was more than just a minor annoyance – it was a limiting factor in her daily life.
