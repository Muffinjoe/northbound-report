10 Podcasts That Will Actually Make You Smarter in 2026

Michael Carrington — Northbound Report |March 25, 2026 |5 min read

10 Great Podcasts for Self-Improvement and Learning

As we dive into the world of self-improvement and learning, podcasts have become an excellent source of valuable information and inspiration. In this article, we'll highlight 10 of the best podcasts covering various topics such as science, business, health, psychology, and storytelling.

Science and Learning

  • Radiolab - This podcast dives into complex scientific topics, exploring the intricacies of human experience and the world around us. From the mysteries of the universe to the intricacies of human emotions, Radiolab will challenge your perspective and broaden your understanding.
  • The TED Radio Hour - Based on the popular TED Talks, this podcast brings together fascinating ideas and stories from experts in various fields, covering topics such as science, technology, and culture. Each episode is a thought-provoking conversation that leaves you inspired and motivated.
  • The Science Podcast - Hosted by scientists and science communicators, this podcast covers a wide range of topics, from breakthroughs in medicine to the latest discoveries in the natural world. It's a great resource for anyone looking to stay up-to-date with the latest scientific advancements.

Business and Career

  • The Tim Ferriss Show - Tim Ferriss, a successful entrepreneur and author, interviews successful people from various fields, digging deep into their habits, routines, and strategies for success. It's a must-listen for anyone looking to improve their career and personal life.
  • How I Built This - Hosted by Guy Raz, this podcast tells the stories of some of the world's most successful companies, including Airbnb, LinkedIn, and Dell. It's a fascinating look at the entrepreneurial spirit and the principles that drive innovation.
  • The GaryVee Audio Experience - Gary Vaynerchuk, a serial entrepreneur and marketing expert, shares his insights and advice on how to succeed in business and life. From social media marketing to personal development, GaryVee's podcast is a treasure trove of valuable information.

Health and Well-being

  • The Model Health Show - Hosted by Shawn Stevenson, this podcast covers various topics related to health and wellness, including nutrition, fitness, and stress management. It's a great resource for anyone looking to improve their physical and mental health.
  • The Mindful Kind - Rachael Kable, a mindfulness expert, shares practical tips and techniques for cultivating mindfulness and reducing stress. Her podcast is a great resource for anyone looking to improve their mental well-being.

Psychology and Personal Growth

  • The Happiness Lab with Dr. Laurie Santos - Based on a popular college course, this podcast explores the science behind happiness and well-being. From the importance of gratitude to the role of social connections, Dr. Santos shares actionable tips and insights for living a happier life.
  • The School of Greatness with Lewis Howes - Lewis Howes, a life coach and motivational speaker, shares his insights and advice on how to achieve greatness in life. From personal development to entrepreneurship, his podcast is a great resource for anyone looking to improve their life.

Storytelling and Inspiration

  • The Moth - This podcast features true stories told live on stage, covering a wide range of topics from love and loss to adventure and triumph. It's a great way to tap into the human experience and find inspiration in the stories of others.
  • Sawbones: A Marital Tour of Misguided Medicine - Hosted by Dr. Sydnee McElroy and her husband Justin, this podcast tells the fascinating stories of medical history, covering topics such as ancient remedies and medical myths.

These 10 podcasts offer a wealth of knowledge and inspiration for anyone looking to improve their life and expand their horizons. Whether you're interested in science, business, health, psychology, or storytelling, there's something here for everyone. So grab your headphones, settle in, and start listening!
